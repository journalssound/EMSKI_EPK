EMSKI Is Already Working. Capital Removes the Bottleneck.

EMSKI is an electronic music project built and operated by Emma — a producer, vocalist, drummer, and visual director. Since launching in 2023 with no label, no outside capital, and no safety net, the project has generated 3.15M streams, 42.3K followers, 7.7M playlist reach, major label releases, festival appearances, and a national tour support slot. All self-funded from film production income.

This is not a bet on potential. It is a proven system constrained by capital.
What's Been Proven

Already Resonating — Not at Scale

EMSKI has signed and released records on some of the biggest labels in electronic music — mau5trap, Ophelia, Monstercat, Bitbird, Hexagon, Sable Valley. She didn't release every track under her own name, but the music has been placed on rosters that most artists spend years trying to reach. That's not a starting point — that's validation.

Alongside the releases: warehouse headliners, festival slots at ILLfest, Freaky Deaky, Seismic, and Big Bond, plus a 20+ date support run on the Zella Day national tour. The audience connection isn't theoretical. It's real, measurable, and happening — it's just not happening at scale because there's no capital behind it.
