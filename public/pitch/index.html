Partnership

How it works

Three tiers, each structured with real upside — revenue sharing, full transparency, and at higher levels, equity in The Effect LLC.

Spark $25K
  • 5% net revenue share on E/MOTION EP — master & publishing streaming royalties, 3 year term
  • Quarterly financial reports — streams, revenue, spend
  • Founding supporter credit on all EP-related releases
  • Monthly update calls — direct access, no filter
Catalyst $75K
  • Everything in Spark, plus:
  • 3% equity stake in The Effect LLC (label/brand entity)
  • 8% net revenue share across all future releases — master & publishing streaming, 5 year term
  • Advisory input on business direction & strategy
  • Name featured in launch campaign & liner credits
Architect $150K+
  • Everything in Catalyst, plus:
  • 7% equity stake in The Effect LLC
  • 12% net revenue share across all releases — master & publishing streaming, sync, brand partnerships, 7 year term
  • Pro-rata rights on any future funding rounds
  • Annual in-person strategy session + full financial audit access

All terms are negotiable. This is a starting framework — the real structure gets built in conversation.
