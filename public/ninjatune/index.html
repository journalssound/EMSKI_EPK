Strategy & Marketing

Content Strategy

Four Pillar Approach

Every track on the e/MOTION EP is accompanied by a suite of visual content designed for multi-platform rollout across Spotify Canvas, Instagram Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts. The content is organized around four strategic pillars:

01
High-Quality Production
Cinematic lyric videos, visualizers, and full music videos shot and edited with professional production value. Every visual matches the sonic identity.
02
Live Performance
High-energy show clips, crowd footage, festival moments, and live edits that showcase stage presence and audience engagement.
03
Casual Content
Lo-fi, spontaneous moments — studio sessions, daily life, tour clips, and candid content that feels real and relatable. Volume over polish.
04
Talking Head
Authentic, unfiltered direct-to-camera content — behind-the-scenes process, production breakdowns, and personal storytelling that builds parasocial connection.
